About Energy Vampire Blasters

Energy Vampire Blasters is a personal development brand built around a simple, powerful idea: most people are being drained by toxic relationships, negative people, bad habits, and destructive self-talk -- and they can't fix what they can't name.

Founded by David Lloyd Strauss, international bestselling author and creator of the Energy Vampire framework, EVB has helped thousands of people identify the 13 types of Energy Vampires in their lives and take concrete action to reclaim their power.

Through books, online challenges, a private coaching community, and a growing content ecosystem, Energy Vampire Blasters is turning a coaching methodology into a cultural movement.

About David Lloyd Strauss

David Lloyd Strauss is an international bestselling author, professional ghost writer, and the creator of the Energy Vampire framework. He is the founder of Energy Vampire Blasters and the author of Energy Vampire Blasters: 13 Toxic Relationships That Drain Your Energy and How to Blast Them Out of Your Life.

David was mentored by Sharon Lechter, co-author of Rich Dad Poor Dad and advisor to the Napoleon Hill Foundation. He has been endorsed by Russell Brunson and the Napoleon Hill Foundation.

His work has been featured on ABC, NBC, CBS, Fox, NPR, and the United Nations. He speaks internationally on topics of toxic relationships, personal energy management, and gamified personal development.
