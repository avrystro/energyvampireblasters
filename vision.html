Where We're Going

The Vision

Energy Vampire Blasters is becoming a movement. Here's where we're going.

The Mission

We help people identify and remove toxic relationships, negative people, bad habits, and destructive inner dialogue so they can reclaim their energy and build a life that feeds them.

Every person walking around drained, stuck in a loop of toxic love, self-sabotage, or someone else's negativity deserves a framework that names the problem and gives them the weapons to fight back. That's what we're building.

The 10-Year Vision

We're building Energy Vampire Blasters into an all-ages brand with a licensing model inspired by Chicken Soup for the Soul. Books, cartoons, products, events, and a framework that scales across every medium and every audience.

Picture this: a Scooby-Doo style cartoon series where a gang of characters hunt down the 13 Energy Vampires. Product lines in nutrition, merch, and wellness. Seasonal content franchises around Halloween, Valentine's Day, Thanksgiving, and New Year's. Children's programming that teaches kids to name what's draining them before they grow up trapped by it. Licensed IP that turns a coaching framework into a cultural movement.

"Think Chicken Soup for the Soul meets Scooby-Doo. An all-ages brand that makes the invisible visible and gives people the weapons to fight back."

This isn't about building a business. It's about building a brand that outlives any single book, coach, or platform. Energy Vampires are universal. The framework we're creating is the lens that makes them visible and the toolkit that makes them beatable.

The 3-Year Picture

Within three years, we're targeting $20K in monthly recurring revenue, 200+ active members in our Skool community, and daily content on Instagram that feeds the movement organically.

We'll have partnerships with coaches, therapists, and influencers who use the Energy Vampire framework in their own work. The first cartoon character designs will be complete. The Energy Vampire Blasters trademark will be filed and protected.

The foundation will be set: a proven framework, a loyal community, a recognizable brand, and the creative assets to scale into entertainment, products, and live events.

The Movement

This isn't just about one book or one coach. We're building a framework that anyone can use to name what's draining them and take action.

Through books, challenges, live coaching, community, and soon -- cartoons, nutrition, events, and merch -- we're creating an ecosystem where people don't just learn about Energy Vampires. They learn how to blast them out of their lives for good.

The Energy Vampire framework is the common language. The community is the support system. The content is the delivery mechanism. And the brand is the signal that tells the world: we don't tolerate energy theft anymore.
