## Day-by-Day Breakdown

### Day 1: Identify Your Vampires
- **Email subject:** "Day 1: Name the vampires draining your life"
- **Lesson:** What are Energy Vampires? The 4 categories (people, situations, addictions, bad habits). Why naming them matters.
- **Exercise:** Make a list of every person, habit, and pattern draining your energy. Don't filter -- write everything.
- **Deliverable:** "My Energy Vampire List" worksheet (fillable PDF)
- **Affirmation:** "I have the courage to see what's been hiding in the dark."
- **VIP Q&A prompt:** "Share your list in the group. David will react to 3 of them live."

### Day 2: Meet the 5 Types
- **Email subject:** "Day 2: Which of these 5 vampires is YOUR biggest drain?"
- **Lesson:** The Taker, The Drama Queen, The Blamer, The Scroll Hole, The Inner Vampire. Quick profiles of each.
- **Exercise:** Match every item on yesterday's list to one of the 5 types. Circle the type that shows up most.
- **Deliverable:** "Vampire Type Matcher" one-page guide
- **Affirmation:** "Now that I can see them, they can no longer hide."
- **VIP Q&A prompt:** "Which type dominated your list? David breaks down what it means."

### Day 3: Find Your Inner Vampire
- **Email subject:** "Day 3: The vampire you didn't see coming (it's you)"
- **Lesson:** The Inner Vampire -- negative self-talk, self-sabotage, victim mindset. The 15 signs. Why every external vampire feeds off your Inner Vampire.
- **Exercise:** Inner dialogue audit. For one full day, write down every negative thing you say to yourself. Catch the chatter.
- **Deliverable:** "Inner Vampire Chatter Log" worksheet
- **Affirmation:** "I am not my negative thoughts. I am the one who notices them."
- **VIP Q&A prompt:** "Read your harshest piece of inner chatter to the group. David reframes it live."

### Day 4: Walls & Bridges
- **Email subject:** "Day 4: A 4-minute exercise that will change how you see yourself"
- **Lesson:** The Walls & Bridges exercise. Wall 1 = limiting beliefs, fears, blame. Wall 2 = strengths, blessings, people who build you up.
- **Exercise:** Fill in both walls (2 minutes each). Compare. Which wall was easier to fill?
- **Deliverable:** Walls & Bridges printable worksheet
- **Affirmation:** "My strengths are real. My fears are learned. I choose which wall I build on."
- **VIP Q&A prompt:** "Which wall had more bricks? David talks about what that reveals."

### Day 5: Set Your Boundaries
- **Email subject:** "Day 5: 'No' is a complete sentence"
- **Lesson:** Why boundaries matter. Why Energy Vampires hate them. How to set them without guilt. 3 boundary scripts.
- **Exercise:** Write 3 boundaries you need to set. For each one, write the exact words you'll say. Practice saying them out loud.
- **Deliverable:** "3 Boundary Scripts" template
- **Affirmation:** "Setting boundaries isn't selfish. It's survival."
- **VIP Q&A prompt:** "Share one boundary you're scared to set. David coaches you through it live."

### Day 6: Build Your Tribe
- **Email subject:** "Day 6: The most important decision you'll ever make"
- **Lesson:** Dream Weavers vs Dream Stealers. Why the people you surround yourself with IS the most important decision. How to identify your 3 uplifters.
- **Exercise:** Name 3 people who build you up. Reach out to one of them today -- text, call, DM. Tell them what they mean to you.
- **Deliverable:** "Dream Weaver Identification" one-page guide
- **Affirmation:** "I deserve to be surrounded by people who make me better."
- **VIP Q&A prompt:** "Who did you reach out to? What happened? Share with the group."

### Day 7: Blast Day
- **Email subject:** "Day 7: Today you make ONE decision that changes everything"
- **Lesson:** Review your vampire list from Day 1. Pick ONE vampire and make a decision about it. Set a boundary. Walk away. Delete the app. Whatever it is -- do it today.
- **Exercise:** Write down the decision. Write down why. Then do it.
- **Deliverable:** "Blast Day Declaration" one-page
- **Affirmation:** "I am no longer a human Capri Sun for the unpleasant people in my life."
- **VIP extras:**
  - David personally grades your Energy Vampire Scorecard
  - Challenge Completion Certificate (shareable on social)
  - Personal voice note from David (60 seconds)
  - Skool community invitation at 50% off first month ($47 instead of $97)
